Demographic Profile:

The user in the business model is a busy person who wants to develop his company so fast. So to achieve his goal, he will have to spend a lot of time to find right resources. From this perspective, the user in this model want to be so updated with the current technology and he will be a person who keeps good connection with social.

By looking into the day-to-day life of user, he will have to spend time to manage his family life and also needed a lot of time in every activities in his office.

End Goals in business model:

As looking in the side business people, whose looking for software developers, it will be hard since there a lot of graduates finishing their course in a year. And it will be very difficult to track the students who needed the specific skill that needed to work in their company. So our product suggests a software, that user can input the tests that really needed to pass for getting the job.

And the user can view the relational results of tests taken by students, So that the user can choose top performing students from the test.

Scenario:

The user can interact with our product in three ways.

The user can input the test cases in to the software at any time he choose.

The other interaction point is at the end of every day, to check the students who take test on that day.If there exit a student who got good result, the user can specifically mark the student, if he wants.

The third interaction point for the user is to view top performers in a month, So he can skip looking the data every single day.
